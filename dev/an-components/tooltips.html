Tooltips

Use tooltips to display auxiliary/complementary information as a hover over.

Hi there! How are you? I have some tooltips for you here.
Tooltips can slide in from left Slide from left! , from above Slide from above! , from below Slide from below! , or from right Slide from right! .
toggle markup 
<!-- Tooltip from Left -->
<div class="tooltip-container">
  Left <!-- You can have any text or HTML content here -->
  <div class="tooltip" flow="left">Slide from left!</div>
</div>

<!-- Tooltip from Above -->
<div class="tooltip-container">
  Above <!-- You can have any text or HTML content here -->
  <div class="tooltip" flow="up">Slide from above!</div>
</div>

<!-- Tooltip from Below -->
<div class="tooltip-container">
  Below <!-- You can have any text or HTML content here -->
  <div class="tooltip" flow="down">Slide from below!</div>
</div>

<!-- Tooltip from Right -->
<div class="tooltip-container">
  Right <!-- You can have any text or HTML content here -->
  <div class="tooltip" flow="right">Slide from right!</div>
</div>
